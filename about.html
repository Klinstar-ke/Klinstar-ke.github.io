About Me

Kenedy Njagi

Mr Kenedy Njagi is a researcher and data scientist who hails from Kenya and a graduate of the university of Abderrahmane Mira Bejaia in Algeria. His academic journey began with an undergraduate degree in statistics and electronic data processing, providing a robust foundation in data analysis and quantitative methods. His passion lies in providing decision support in companies through bridging the gap between theoretical mathematics and practical applications in real world problems. Driven by his interests, he pursued a masters degree in data science and decision support where he has inturn developed strong data modelling and statistical analysis technics aimed at driving a positive contribution in a data-driven environment.
