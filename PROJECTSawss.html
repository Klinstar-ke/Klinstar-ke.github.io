Page 2: Business Demographics

This dashboard breaks down businesses by age, education, gender, and county. Several key patterns emerge:

  • Economic Activity & Age: Most businesses are concentrated in Wholesale and Retail Trade, dominated by owners aged 36 and above, while younger groups (18–25) are underrepresented across most sectors.
  • Geographic Spread: Business activity is highly concentrated in counties such as Nairobi, Mombasa, and Kiambu, with fewer businesses in sparsely populated regions like Turkana and Marsabit.
  • Education Levels: A majority of business owners have only primary or secondary education. College-educated and university-educated business owners are fewer, suggesting businesses are driven more by opportunity and necessity than formal training.
  • Gender Distribution: Female business ownership is strong, with women running slightly more businesses than men. This highlights the vital role of women in entrepreneurship.
