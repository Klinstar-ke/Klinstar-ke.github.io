Overview

Our dataset covers 2,000 small and medium enterprises across Kenya, spanning urban and rural areas...

Key Insights

  • Total Businesses: 2,000 registered businesses across the dataset.
  • Urban vs Rural: Rural businesses slightly dominate at 51%.
  • Average Age of Owners: 34 years old, showing young entrepreneurship drive.
  • Economic Activities: Trade and Agriculture form the backbone of enterprise activity.
